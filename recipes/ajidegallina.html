Aji de Gallina

The recipe consists of some chicken with a crema de aji de gallina

It's relatively easy to cook for most moms in Peru, I bet their recipe is perfect for their household.

Ingredients

  • aji amarillo
  • chicken
  • su arrocito como no
  • sal
  • zanahoria
  • papa amarilla

Steps

  1. cook the aji amarillo
  2. make el arrocito
  3. add cebolla y zanahoria
  4. licuar para que quede sabroso
  5. hervir pechuga de pollo
  6. desilachar pollo
  7. add pollo y mezcla de licuadora a olla
  8. mezclar hasta queq uede sabroso
